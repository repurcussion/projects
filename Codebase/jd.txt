Job Title: Senior Machine Learning Engineer

Company: InnovateTech AI Labs
Location: Remote / San Francisco, CA
Employment Type: Full-Time

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are seeking a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI Platform team.
You will design, build, and deploy production ML systems with a focus on NLP and
large language models. You will work closely with research scientists, product
managers, and platform engineers to bring cutting-edge AI capabilities to millions
of users.

REQUIRED SKILLS
- Python (advanced proficiency)
- PyTorch or TensorFlow (deep learning frameworks)
- Hugging Face Transformers
- LangChain
- MLflow or similar experiment tracking
- Docker and Kubernetes (containerisation and orchestration)
- AWS or GCP (cloud ML platforms)
- SQL and data manipulation
- FastAPI or REST API design
- Git and CI/CD practices

PREFERRED SKILLS
- Experience with LLMs (GPT, LLaMA, Mistral, Gemma)
- Knowledge of RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
- OpenAI API integration
- Apache Spark for large-scale data processing
- MLOps best practices (model monitoring, drift detection)
- Fine-tuning transformer models (LoRA, PEFT)
- Vector databases (Pinecone, FAISS, Chroma)
- Publication or open-source contribution in ML/NLP

MINIMUM EXPERIENCE
4 years of professional experience in machine learning engineering or a closely
related field. At least 2 years working with deep learning frameworks in production
environments.

EDUCATION REQUIREMENT
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Mathematics, or a related
field. Master's or PhD is preferred but not required.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and implement scalable ML pipelines for NLP tasks
- Fine-tune and deploy large language models for production use cases
- Build LangChain-based applications including RAG systems
- Collaborate with research team to productionise experimental models
- Establish MLOps best practices including monitoring and automated retraining
- Mentor junior engineers and conduct code reviews

WHAT WE OFFER
- Competitive salary ($180,000 – $240,000)
